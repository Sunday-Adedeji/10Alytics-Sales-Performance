## üîç Key Insights
1Ô∏è. Profit Trend

üìå Profit surges sharply entering January 2016, peaks around April‚ÄìMay, then dips slightly mid-year.
This pattern indicates seasonal buying power‚Äîlikely aligned to holiday periods, restocking cycles, or consumer financing patterns.

2Ô∏è. Product Category Analysis
Phones generate the highest profit across the dataset, far exceeding Clothing and Accessories.
Phones are the company's core revenue driver. Low-margin categories like Accessories must be paired as bundles or optimised for cost reduction.

3Ô∏è. Age Group Profitability
Adults and Youth dominate profit contribution.
Marketing should prioritise these age segments, targeting the two age groups for more revenue generation.
Product development can tailor offerings (e.g., trendy items for Youth, functional for Adults).

4Ô∏è. Gender Distribution
Male ‚Üí 52% of profit
Female ‚Üí 48% of profit
Customer base is balanced. Marketing should remain gender-diverse and inclusive.

5Ô∏è. State-Level Performance
Lagos, Abuja, and Akwa Ibom consistently outperform others.
These are high-value markets with strong purchasing power.
Optimal for inventory focus, expansion, and geo-targeted promotions.

6Ô∏è. Salesperson Performance
Filters reveal strong performance from:
Chinazam, Remota and Feyisola
Top reps either manage premium customers or excel in high-margin product sales.
Sales process analysis can help replicate their methods across the team.
